Welcome To My Page

I’m a Full-Stack Developer instilled with a strong work ethic by an early entrance to the workforce. Having studied finance at a top-flight university and possessing seven years experience in the fast-paced environment of the airline industry’s maintenance sector, I’m always ready for the next challenge. I'm a self-learner that believes in lifelong improvement through hard work. Whether that be teaching myself Spanish to fluency, traveling the world over to learn about culture first hand, analyzing music theory or how to code, once I find a passion I do not quit until I see it through to its fruition. All of these past life experiences have led me to pursue a lifelong career within a sector that truly excites me. Through my ceaseless searching, I have finally found a field that offers a rich abundance of learning with the unlimited possibilities that are inherent to technology. In order to accomplish this goal, I studied at The University of Denver’s Full-Stack Coding Bootcamp and have dedicated myself to a myriad of additional online extracurricular resources in order to position myself advantageously to make this goal a reality.
